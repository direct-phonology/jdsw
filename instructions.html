Annotation categories

Span types

  • PHON

    Phonetic information, including fanqie, "reads like", "如字", etc.

  • SEM

    Semantic information or explanations, usually ending in "也".

  • GRAF

    Notes about how a character is graphically represented or omitted, usually including "作" or "無".

  • META

    Qualifiers or quantifiers added to other types of information, sometimes indicating the coverage or applicability of the note, often ending in "同".

  • WORK

    Titles of books, chapters, poems, etc.

  • PER

    People identified by name: given, family, courtesy, etc.

    • Relation types

  • SRC

    Connects a WORK or PER to the information sourced from it, often marked by "云".

  • MOD

    Connects a META to the information that it modifies.
